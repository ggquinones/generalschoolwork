He smiled understandingly much more than understandingly
was one of those rare smiles with a quality of eternal reassurance 
in that you may come across four or five times in life It face or
seemed to face the whole eternal world for an instant and then
concentrated on you with an irresistible prejudice in your favor
It understood you just as far as you wanted to be understood believed
in you as you would like to believe in yourself and assured you that it
had precisely the impression of you that at your best you hoped to convey
