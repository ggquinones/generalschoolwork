Format
Rules

4 vs 4 Grass Volleyball on a standard beach doubles court

- Teams can have a maximum of 5 players.

- A standard beach doubles court will be used. (Small Court)

Pool Play

- Pools of 3 to 5 teams will be made.

- Matches will consist of 2 sets to 21 (Cap at 25)

Single Elimination Playoffs

- Top 8 teams will pass onto playoffs.

Indoor Rules except:

- No open hand tips!

- Only serving order matters, no actual rotations.
