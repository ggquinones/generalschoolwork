TOURNAMENT INFO.

Format
Rules

Men's 3 vs 3 Grass Volleyball on a standard beach doubles court

- Teams can have a maximum of 4 players.

Level of Play: Competitve Men's 3s

The tournament format will be based on the number of teams which register.
Varying formats shown below.

4 or 5 Teams:

- Round robin: 2 sets to 15 (No Cap)

followed by

- Single elimination playoffs: Best 2 out of 3 sets.
First 2 to 21 ,3rd to 15. (No Cap)

6 or 7 Teams:

- Pool Play: 2 sets to 15 (No Cap)

followed by

- Single elimination playoffs: Best 2 out of 3 sets.
First 2 to 21 ,3rd to 15. (No Cap)

8 Teams or more:

- Double elimination tournament: Best 2 out of 3 sets.
First 2 to 21 ,3rd to 15. (No Cap)

Indoor Rules Except:

- No open hand tips!

- No let serve.

- No overhand pass on first contact.

- Except for hard-driven hits.

No rotations

- Only serving order matters.
