Advancement Team
CBVC “A” (Advancement) Team is for players age 12-25 who are interested in learning proper technique and becoming a better volleyball player. CBVC is also for players looking to build strength and skills to help their indoor game. Our system believes in teaching the advanced skills from the beginning of a player’s career. For example, hand setting; even though the player may choose to bump set in tournaments, they should be hand setting in practice to master the skill for the future. “A” Teams will play in all of the same tournaments as the Select teams with the exception of the open level tournaments. Practices will teach the proper skill techniques, mental toughness and proper decision making. As players demonstrate these abilities, they will be invited to play on the Select Team. The advantage of going through the A Team is that the player is learning proper techniques and are constantly under CBVC coaching supervision to be moved up to the Select Team. Select Team practices are more strategy based compared to A Team practices, which are more skill based. CBVC understands players develop at different rates, so all players are accepted. CBVC believes in installing confidence in new players instead of making cuts like other programs. Although we expect these players to work hard, practices will not be as intense as Select Team practices due to differences in age groups and physical fitness levels. Practices will be twice a week plus tournaments on the weekends. All players will receive a ball and uniform.
Select Team
To make the CBVC Select Team, players must consistently demonstrate the ability to make the right decisions each play, be mentally ready, physically fit, have the drive to play at a higher level, and a winning attitude. Players must also take a short written test to ensure each player has completely learned the necessary skills and strategies to play on the select team. Players selected for the CBVC Select Team will receive customized coaching techniques to fit their style of game that will give them the best chance of winning. For example; a shorter player will build their game around defense and the sideout game. A tall player might build theirs around blocking.Every player needs something a little different. CBVC Select will utilize our practice time to isolate each player’s needs. CBVC Select teams will actually compete in qualifiers before the tournaments. Top finishing teams will receive covered entry fees for that particular weekend tournament. This gives the players the same feel and pressures as going through a professional qualifier. Players will also receive their own ball and autographed uniform. There are many privileges to being selected to the CBVC Select team, but we expect hard work in return. CBVC Select is intended only for players ages 16-20 that not only possess the athletic ability but the desire to play at a higher level. This program is not for players that are just playing for the enjoyment. Practices are extremely strenuous and we work hard on becoming a better player. CBVC will only tolerate winning and positive attitudes. We want you to learn how to win humbly and lose gracefully. Practices will be used efficiently. Practice is not a place to share the recent gossip or chat with your girlfriends. If this is you, you have been warned! All players must start on the CBVC Advancement team before advancing to CBVC Select. The coaching staff has the ability to bring advanced players directly into program. CBVC ran tournaments will have experienced referees in their final matches to ensure the match is correctly and fairly played. We will encourage hand setting and want players that feel comfortable with the skill to use it without the worry of the ref calling incorrect lifts and double contacts. CBVC Select teams will also be encouraged to compete in the open level tournaments. These are the same tourneys that the coaches play. This will be the highest level of coaching in Texas and we are excited about all the new future professionals to come!

ChampionBVC Training Stages

Stage One

We determine your skill level and playing experience. Based on this, we establish the style of learning that is most responsive to you and what you will benefit the most from. We make our assessment, adjust our method to suit your style, then we focus on what skills need the most attention. Having played and experienced almost every facet of Beach Volleyball, we have nearly a dozen ways of explaining each and every skill performed in great detail and in ways each individual will grasp and get full benefit from. It's just a matter of finding the right way for each person to respond accordingly.

Stage Two

We create and customize your training to fit your individual skills, needs, and wants. We always start with fundamentals and break them down first, as fundamentals are the basis for every action that we do. We try to make the game simple and easy to understand, allowing you to be as effective and efficient as possible. We like to video tape the training session (if package is purchased) so that the student can see themselves perform skills and situations. This has been a great training aid in gaining rapid progress.

Stage Three

We motivate through goal accomplishment and positive feedback while pressing the importance of technique, ball control, efficient movement, and high repetitions. In this stage, we focus on the little picture while explaining its role in the big picture. Practice doesn't make perfect: Perfect Practice makes perfect.

Stage Four

We train your muscle memory through "perfect practice" one step at a time until that fundamental, body movement, or skill becomes fluid and thoughtless. During this process, we'll keep you aware of the game situation that you are practicing in order to keep everything relative and concentrated, which also allows you to perform the skill or movement without putting any thought to it. Our goal is to ensure that each movement is a "reaction" and with controlled thought. Frequently we video tape the workout to allow you to view your improvements and technique frame by frame. We offer a full DVD training package for you to take your lessons home with you to study and learn from.
