CBVC is the premier beach volleyball and fitness training program designed to help aspiring volleyball players achieve their volleyball or athletic goals, to play professionally, or to just become a better all around volleyball player or athlete. CBVC was started as a vision of Skyler "Dale" Davis and Phil Dalhausser many years ago while playing on the beaches in Florida and South Carolina. As time has passed and their lives have grown, that dream was once more discussed as a necessity to help create a developmental program teaching the correct way to play beach volleyball through the highest quality coaching available on "real" beaches...or something as close to them as possible. The Champion coaching staff are all avid beach players who can be found playing most days at the Beach Ellum. All of our coaches are still competitive players, and their bios and playing abilities are put on display at almost every professional tournament around. Our coaches are proud of their accomplishments as their work ethic and knowledge of the game of beach volleyball has elevated them to the biggest stages. If you haven't won money on the tour, then you haven't made it "AVP".. The coaching staff at CBVC are all knowledgeable beach players as well as multiple sport athletes. Our coaching staff still competes in open tournaments and AVP events and their videos and games can be watched online and in person!
